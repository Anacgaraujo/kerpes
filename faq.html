Shipping:
Yes, we will ship your firearm to another FFL holder!  We charge the actual shipping cost plus $15 for processing.  Please do not have your firearm pre-packaged as we will need to verify the serial number.
Please have the receiving FFL holder email a copy of their license to us at “kerpers@hotmail.com“. Be sure they include your name, the buyers name and type of firearm.

We use USPS for handgun shipments and FedEx or UPS for long gun shipments. If you have a preference, please specify which carrier you prefer when dropping off your firearm. Go to “HOME” page
